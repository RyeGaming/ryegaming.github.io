About Brian


Brian Hudick is a junior technical artist specializing in both
3D prop and character creation as well as animation rig building
for video games and realtime applications. With education in Computer
Science and Game Design from the University of California, Santa Cruz
and preproduction experience under Tech Guide Blogs, he's ready for production.

Working as a student assistance specialist under UCSC's Veterans' Resource
center while studying for his bachelor's degree, he's graduating in June 2026.
He's seeking to break into the business to create stylized characters and props.
Heavily experienced in Unity, Maya, Substance Painter 3D, and of course Blender,
he understands the production pipeline of assets to make the creation and
implementation from sculpt to gameplay as smooth as can be. Brian is
Currently operating out of Santa Cruz, CA.
