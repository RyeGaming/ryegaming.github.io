I'm a Filipino-American originally born in Austin, TX before my family moved out to Pasadena, CA 4 years later, where I grew up.
Growing up under two United States Army veterans, I was raised with the mindset of duty above all: duty to your people, your craft, and yourself.
When COVID hit in high school, I began to learn how to use Blender, the software of choice for every indie 3D model artist. Since then, I've been pursuing every aspect of the craft whenever I can.
Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Game Design and building an isometric action game for my capstone project.
My field of expertise is the creation of 3D props and characters and prepping them for texturing, animation, and gameplay implementation. I extend myself towards multiple works in order to build a higher understanding of what makes each style appeal to its enjoyers.
